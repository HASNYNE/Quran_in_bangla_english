Chapter - 96: Al-Alaq

Verse - 1: Recite in the name of your Lord who created -
Verse - 2: Created man from a clinging substance.
Verse - 3: Recite, and your Lord is the most Generous -
Verse - 4: Who taught by the pen -
Verse - 5: Taught man that which he knew not.
Verse - 6: No! [But] indeed, man transgresses
Verse - 7: Because he sees himself self-sufficient.
Verse - 8: Indeed, to your Lord is the return.
Verse - 9: Have you seen the one who forbids
Verse - 10: A servant when he prays?
Verse - 11: Have you seen if he is upon guidance
Verse - 12: Or enjoins righteousness?
Verse - 13: Have you seen if he denies and turns away -
Verse - 14: Does he not know that Allah sees?
Verse - 15: No! If he does not desist, We will surely drag him by the forelock -
Verse - 16: A lying, sinning forelock.
Verse - 17: Then let him call his associates;
Verse - 18: We will call the angels of Hell.
Verse - 19: No! Do not obey him. But prostrate and draw near [to Allah].
