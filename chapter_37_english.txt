Chapter - 37: As-Saaffaat

Verse - 1: By those [angels] lined up in rows
Verse - 2: And those who drive [the clouds]
Verse - 3: And those who recite the message,
Verse - 4: Indeed, your God is One,
Verse - 5: Lord of the heavens and the earth and that between them and Lord of the sunrises.
Verse - 6: Indeed, We have adorned the nearest heaven with an adornment of stars
Verse - 7: And as protection against every rebellious devil
Verse - 8: [So] they may not listen to the exalted assembly [of angels] and are pelted from every side,
Verse - 9: Repelled; and for them is a constant punishment,
Verse - 10: Except one who snatches [some words] by theft, but they are pursued by a burning flame, piercing [in brightness].
Verse - 11: Then inquire of them, [O Muhammad], "Are they a stronger [or more difficult] creation or those [others] We have created?" Indeed, We created men from sticky clay.
Verse - 12: But you wonder, while they mock,
Verse - 13: And when they are reminded, they remember not.
Verse - 14: And when they see a sign, they ridicule
Verse - 15: And say, "This is not but obvious magic.
Verse - 16: When we have died and become dust and bones, are we indeed to be resurrected?
Verse - 17: And our forefathers [as well]?"
Verse - 18: Say, "Yes, and you will be [rendered] contemptible."
Verse - 19: It will be only one shout, and at once they will be observing.
Verse - 20: They will say, "O woe to us! This is the Day of Recompense."
Verse - 21: [They will be told], "This is the Day of Judgement which you used to deny."
Verse - 22: [The angels will be ordered], "Gather those who committed wrong, their kinds, and what they used to worship
Verse - 23: Other than Allah, and guide them to the path of Hellfire
Verse - 24: And stop them; indeed, they are to be questioned."
Verse - 25: [They will be asked], "What is [wrong] with you? Why do you not help each other?"
Verse - 26: But they, that Day, are in surrender.
Verse - 27: And they will approach one another blaming each other.
Verse - 28: They will say, "Indeed, you used to come at us from the right."
Verse - 29: The oppressors will say, "Rather, you [yourselves] were not believers,
Verse - 30: And we had over you no authority, but you were a transgressing people.
Verse - 31: So the word of our Lord has come into effect upon us; indeed, we will taste [punishment].
Verse - 32: And we led you to deviation; indeed, we were deviators."
Verse - 33: So indeed they, that Day, will be sharing in the punishment.
Verse - 34: Indeed, that is how We deal with the criminals.
Verse - 35: Indeed they, when it was said to them, "There is no deity but Allah," were arrogant
Verse - 36: And were saying, "Are we to leave our gods for a mad poet?"
Verse - 37: Rather, the Prophet has come with the truth and confirmed the [previous] messengers.
Verse - 38: Indeed, you [disbelievers] will be tasters of the painful punishment,
Verse - 39: And you will not be recompensed except for what you used to do -
Verse - 40: But not the chosen servants of Allah.
Verse - 41: Those will have a provision determined -
Verse - 42: Fruits; and they will be honored
Verse - 43: In gardens of pleasure
Verse - 44: On thrones facing one another.
Verse - 45: There will be circulated among them a cup [of wine] from a flowing spring,
Verse - 46: White and delicious to the drinkers;
Verse - 47: No bad effect is there in it, nor from it will they be intoxicated.
Verse - 48: And with them will be women limiting [their] glances, with large, [beautiful] eyes,
Verse - 49: As if they were [delicate] eggs, well-protected.
Verse - 50: And they will approach one another, inquiring of each other.
Verse - 51: A speaker among them will say, "Indeed, I had a companion [on earth]
Verse - 52: Who would say, 'Are you indeed of those who believe
Verse - 53: That when we have died and become dust and bones, we will indeed be recompensed?'"
Verse - 54: He will say, "Would you [care to] look?"
Verse - 55: And he will look and see him in the midst of the Hellfire.
Verse - 56: He will say, "By Allah, you almost ruined me.
Verse - 57: If not for the favor of my Lord, I would have been of those brought in [to Hell].
Verse - 58: Then, are we not to die
Verse - 59: Except for our first death, and we will not be punished?"
Verse - 60: Indeed, this is the great attainment.
Verse - 61: For the like of this let the workers [on earth] work.
Verse - 62: Is Paradise a better accommodation or the tree of zaqqum?
Verse - 63: Indeed, We have made it a torment for the wrongdoers.
Verse - 64: Indeed, it is a tree issuing from the bottom of the Hellfire,
Verse - 65: Its emerging fruit as if it was heads of the devils.
Verse - 66: And indeed, they will eat from it and fill with it their bellies.
Verse - 67: Then indeed, they will have after it a mixture of scalding water.
Verse - 68: Then indeed, their return will be to the Hellfire.
Verse - 69: Indeed they found their fathers astray.
Verse - 70: So they hastened [to follow] in their footsteps.
Verse - 71: And there had already strayed before them most of the former peoples,
Verse - 72: And We had already sent among them warners.
Verse - 73: Then look how was the end of those who were warned -
Verse - 74: But not the chosen servants of Allah.
Verse - 75: And Noah had certainly called Us, and [We are] the best of responders.
Verse - 76: And We saved him and his family from the great affliction.
Verse - 77: And We made his descendants those remaining [on the earth]
Verse - 78: And left for him [favorable mention] among later generations:
Verse - 79: "Peace upon Noah among the worlds."
Verse - 80: Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.
Verse - 81: Indeed, he was of Our believing servants.
Verse - 82: Then We drowned the disbelievers.
Verse - 83: And indeed, among his kind was Abraham,
Verse - 84: When he came to his Lord with a sound heart
Verse - 85: [And] when he said to his father and his people, "What do you worship?
Verse - 86: Is it falsehood [as] gods other than Allah you desire?
Verse - 87: Then what is your thought about the Lord of the worlds?"
Verse - 88: And he cast a look at the stars
Verse - 89: And said, "Indeed, I am [about to be] ill."
Verse - 90: So they turned away from him, departing.
Verse - 91: Then he turned to their gods and said, "Do you not eat?
Verse - 92: What is [wrong] with you that you do not speak?"
Verse - 93: And he turned upon them a blow with [his] right hand.
Verse - 94: Then the people came toward him, hastening.
Verse - 95: He said, "Do you worship that which you [yourselves] carve,
Verse - 96: While Allah created you and that which you do?"
Verse - 97: They said, "Construct for him a furnace and throw him into the burning fire."
Verse - 98: And they intended for him a plan, but We made them the most debased.
Verse - 99: And [then] he said, "Indeed, I will go to [where I am ordered by] my Lord; He will guide me.
Verse - 100: My Lord, grant me [a child] from among the righteous."
Verse - 101: So We gave him good tidings of a forbearing boy.
Verse - 102: And when he reached with him [the age of] exertion, he said, "O my son, indeed I have seen in a dream that I [must] sacrifice you, so see what you think." He said, "O my father, do as you are commanded. You will find me, if Allah wills, of the steadfast."
Verse - 103: And when they had both submitted and he put him down upon his forehead,
Verse - 104: We called to him, "O Abraham,
Verse - 105: You have fulfilled the vision." Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.
Verse - 106: Indeed, this was the clear trial.
Verse - 107: And We ransomed him with a great sacrifice,
Verse - 108: And We left for him [favorable mention] among later generations:
Verse - 109: "Peace upon Abraham."
Verse - 110: Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.
Verse - 111: Indeed, he was of Our believing servants.
Verse - 112: And We gave him good tidings of Isaac, a prophet from among the righteous.
Verse - 113: And We blessed him and Isaac. But among their descendants is the doer of good and the clearly unjust to himself.
Verse - 114: And We did certainly confer favor upon Moses and Aaron.
Verse - 115: And We saved them and their people from the great affliction,
Verse - 116: And We supported them so it was they who overcame.
Verse - 117: And We gave them the explicit Scripture,
Verse - 118: And We guided them on the straight path.
Verse - 119: And We left for them [favorable mention] among later generations:
Verse - 120: "Peace upon Moses and Aaron."
Verse - 121: Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.
Verse - 122: Indeed, they were of Our believing servants.
Verse - 123: And indeed, Elias was from among the messengers,
Verse - 124: When he said to his people, "Will you not fear Allah?
Verse - 125: Do you call upon Ba'l and leave the best of creators -
Verse - 126: Allah, your Lord and the Lord of your first forefathers?"
Verse - 127: And they denied him, so indeed, they will be brought [for punishment],
Verse - 128: Except the chosen servants of Allah.
Verse - 129: And We left for him [favorable mention] among later generations:
Verse - 130: "Peace upon Elias."
Verse - 131: Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.
Verse - 132: Indeed, he was of Our believing servants.
Verse - 133: And indeed, Lot was among the messengers.
Verse - 134: [So mention] when We saved him and his family, all,
Verse - 135: Except his wife among those who remained [with the evildoers].
Verse - 136: Then We destroyed the others.
Verse - 137: And indeed, you pass by them in the morning
Verse - 138: And at night. Then will you not use reason?
Verse - 139: And indeed, Jonah was among the messengers.
Verse - 140: [Mention] when he ran away to the laden ship.
Verse - 141: And he drew lots and was among the losers.
Verse - 142: Then the fish swallowed him, while he was blameworthy.
Verse - 143: And had he not been of those who exalt Allah,
Verse - 144: He would have remained inside its belly until the Day they are resurrected.
Verse - 145: But We threw him onto the open shore while he was ill.
Verse - 146: And We caused to grow over him a gourd vine.
Verse - 147: And We sent him to [his people of] a hundred thousand or more.
Verse - 148: And they believed, so We gave them enjoyment [of life] for a time.
Verse - 149: So inquire of them, [O Muhammad], "Does your Lord have daughters while they have sons?
Verse - 150: Or did We create the angels as females while they were witnesses?"
Verse - 151: Unquestionably, it is out of their [invented] falsehood that they say,
Verse - 152: " Allah has begotten," and indeed, they are liars.
Verse - 153: Has He chosen daughters over sons?
Verse - 154: What is [wrong] with you? How do you make judgement?
Verse - 155: Then will you not be reminded?
Verse - 156: Or do you have a clear authority?
Verse - 157: Then produce your scripture, if you should be truthful.
Verse - 158: And they have claimed between Him and the jinn a lineage, but the jinn have already known that they [who made such claims] will be brought to [punishment].

Verse - 160: Except the chosen servants of Allah [who do not share in that sin].
Verse - 161: So indeed, you [disbelievers] and whatever you worship,
Verse - 162: You cannot tempt [anyone] away from Him
Verse - 163: Except he who is to [enter and] burn in the Hellfire.
Verse - 164: [The angels say], "There is not among us any except that he has a known position.
Verse - 165: And indeed, we are those who line up [for prayer].
Verse - 166: And indeed, we are those who exalt Allah."

Verse - 168: "If we had a message from [those of] the former peoples,

Verse - 172: [That] indeed, they would be those given victory
