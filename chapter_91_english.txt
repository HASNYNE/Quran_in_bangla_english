Chapter - 91: Ash-Shams

Verse - 1: By the sun and its brightness
Verse - 2: And [by] the moon when it follows it
Verse - 3: And [by] the day when it displays it
Verse - 4: And [by] the night when it covers it
Verse - 5: And [by] the sky and He who constructed it
Verse - 6: And [by] the earth and He who spread it
Verse - 7: And [by] the soul and He who proportioned it
Verse - 8: And inspired it [with discernment of] its wickedness and its righteousness,
Verse - 9: He has succeeded who purifies it,
Verse - 10: And he has failed who instills it [with corruption].
Verse - 11: Thamud denied [their prophet] by reason of their transgression,
Verse - 12: When the most wretched of them was sent forth.
Verse - 13: And the messenger of Allah [Salih] said to them, "[Do not harm] the she-camel of Allah or [prevent her from] her drink."
Verse - 14: But they denied him and hamstrung her. So their Lord brought down upon them destruction for their sin and made it equal [upon all of them].
Verse - 15: And He does not fear the consequence thereof.
