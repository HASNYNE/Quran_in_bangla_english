Chapter - 15: Al-Hijr

Verse - 1: Alif, Lam, Ra. These are the verses of the Book and a clear Qur'an.
Verse - 2: Perhaps those who disbelieve will wish that they had been Muslims.
Verse - 3: Let them eat and enjoy themselves and be diverted by [false] hope, for they are going to know.
Verse - 4: And We did not destroy any city but that for it was a known decree.
Verse - 5: No nation will precede its term, nor will they remain thereafter.
Verse - 6: And they say, "O you upon whom the message has been sent down, indeed you are mad.
Verse - 7: Why do you not bring us the angels, if you should be among the truthful?"
Verse - 8: We do not send down the angels except with truth; and the disbelievers would not then be reprieved.
Verse - 9: Indeed, it is We who sent down the Qur'an and indeed, We will be its guardian.
Verse - 10: And We had certainly sent [messengers] before you, [O Muhammad], among the sects of the former peoples.
Verse - 11: And no messenger would come to them except that they ridiculed him.
Verse - 12: Thus do We insert denial into the hearts of the criminals.
Verse - 13: They will not believe in it, while there has already occurred the precedent of the former peoples.
Verse - 14: And [even] if We opened to them a gate from the heaven and they continued therein to ascend,
Verse - 15: They would say, "Our eyes have only been dazzled. Rather, we are a people affected by magic."
Verse - 16: And We have placed within the heaven great stars and have beautified it for the observers.
Verse - 17: And We have protected it from every devil expelled [from the mercy of Allah]
Verse - 18: Except one who steals a hearing and is pursued by a clear burning flame.
Verse - 19: And the earth - We have spread it and cast therein firmly set mountains and caused to grow therein [something] of every well-balanced thing.
Verse - 20: And We have made for you therein means of living and [for] those for whom you are not providers.
Verse - 21: And there is not a thing but that with Us are its depositories, and We do not send it down except according to a known measure.
Verse - 22: And We have sent the fertilizing winds and sent down water from the sky and given you drink from it. And you are not its retainers.
Verse - 23: And indeed, it is We who give life and cause death, and We are the Inheritor.
Verse - 24: And We have already known the preceding [generations] among you, and We have already known the later [ones to come].
Verse - 25: And indeed, your Lord will gather them; indeed, He is Wise and Knowing.
Verse - 26: And We did certainly create man out of clay from an altered black mud.
Verse - 27: And the jinn We created before from scorching fire.
Verse - 28: And [mention, O Muhammad], when your Lord said to the angels, "I will create a human being out of clay from an altered black mud.
Verse - 29: And when I have proportioned him and breathed into him of My [created] soul, then fall down to him in prostration."
Verse - 30: So the angels prostrated - all of them entirely,
Verse - 31: Except Iblees, he refused to be with those who prostrated.
Verse - 32: [Allah] said, O Iblees, what is [the matter] with you that you are not with those who prostrate?"
Verse - 33: He said, "Never would I prostrate to a human whom You created out of clay from an altered black mud."
Verse - 34: [Allah] said, "Then get out of it, for indeed, you are expelled.
Verse - 35: And indeed, upon you is the curse until the Day of Recompense."
Verse - 36: He said, "My Lord, then reprieve me until the Day they are resurrected."
Verse - 37: [Allah] said, "So indeed, you are of those reprieved
Verse - 38: Until the Day of the time well-known."
Verse - 39: [Iblees] said, "My Lord, because You have put me in error, I will surely make [disobedience] attractive to them on earth, and I will mislead them all
Verse - 40: Except, among them, Your chosen servants."
Verse - 41: [Allah] said, "This is a path [of return] to Me [that is] straight.
Verse - 42: Indeed, My servants - no authority will you have over them, except those who follow you of the deviators.
Verse - 43: And indeed, Hell is the promised place for them all.
Verse - 44: It has seven gates; for every gate is of them a portion designated."
Verse - 45: Indeed, the righteous will be within gardens and springs.
Verse - 46: [Having been told], "Enter it in peace, safe [and secure]."
Verse - 47: And We will remove whatever is in their breasts of resentment, [so they will be] brothers, on thrones facing each other.
Verse - 48: No fatigue will touch them therein, nor from it will they [ever] be removed.
Verse - 49: [O Muhammad], inform My servants that it is I who am the Forgiving, the Merciful.
Verse - 50: And that it is My punishment which is the painful punishment.
Verse - 51: And inform them about the guests of Abraham,
Verse - 52: When they entered upon him and said, "Peace." [Abraham] said, "Indeed, we are fearful of you."
Verse - 53: [The angels] said, "Fear not. Indeed, we give you good tidings of a learned boy."
Verse - 54: He said, "Have you given me good tidings although old age has come upon me? Then of what [wonder] do you inform?"
Verse - 55: They said, "We have given you good tidings in truth, so do not be of the despairing."
Verse - 56: He said, "And who despairs of the mercy of his Lord except for those astray?"
Verse - 57: [Abraham] said, "Then what is your business [here], O messengers?"
Verse - 58: They said, "Indeed, we have been sent to a people of criminals,
Verse - 59: Except the family of Lot; indeed, we will save them all
Verse - 60: Except his wife." Allah decreed that she is of those who remain behind.
Verse - 61: And when the messengers came to the family of Lot,
Verse - 62: He said, "Indeed, you are people unknown."
Verse - 63: They said, "But we have come to you with that about which they were disputing,
Verse - 64: And we have come to you with truth, and indeed, we are truthful.
Verse - 65: So set out with your family during a portion of the night and follow behind them and let not anyone among you look back and continue on to where you are commanded."
Verse - 66: And We conveyed to him [the decree] of that matter: that those [sinners] would be eliminated by early morning.
Verse - 67: And the people of the city came rejoicing.
Verse - 68: [Lot] said, "Indeed, these are my guests, so do not shame me.
Verse - 69: And fear Allah and do not disgrace me."
Verse - 70: They said, "Have we not forbidden you from [protecting] people?"
Verse - 71: [Lot] said, "These are my daughters - if you would be doers [of lawful marriage]."
Verse - 72: By your life, [O Muhammad], indeed they were, in their intoxication, wandering blindly.
Verse - 73: So the shriek seized them at sunrise.
Verse - 74: And We made the highest part [of the city] its lowest and rained upon them stones of hard clay.
Verse - 75: Indeed in that are signs for those who discern.
Verse - 76: And indeed, those cities are [situated] on an established road.
Verse - 77: Indeed in that is a sign for the believers.
Verse - 78: And the companions of the thicket were [also] wrongdoers.
Verse - 79: So We took retribution from them, and indeed, both [cities] are on a clear highway.
Verse - 80: And certainly did the companions of Thamud deny the messengers.
Verse - 81: And We gave them Our signs, but from them they were turning away.
Verse - 82: And they used to carve from the mountains, houses, feeling secure.
Verse - 83: But the shriek seized them at early morning.
Verse - 84: So nothing availed them [from] what they used to earn.
Verse - 85: And We have not created the heavens and earth and that between them except in truth. And indeed, the Hour is coming; so forgive with gracious forgiveness.
Verse - 86: Indeed, your Lord - He is the Knowing Creator.
Verse - 87: And We have certainly given you, [O Muhammad], seven of the often repeated [verses] and the great Qur'an.
Verse - 88: Do not extend your eyes toward that by which We have given enjoyment to [certain] categories of the disbelievers, and do not grieve over them. And lower your wing to the believers
Verse - 89: And say, "Indeed, I am the clear warner" -
Verse - 90: Just as We had revealed [scriptures] to the separators
Verse - 91: Who have made the Qur'an into portions.
Verse - 92: So by your Lord, We will surely question them all
Verse - 93: About what they used to do.
Verse - 94: Then declare what you are commanded and turn away from the polytheists.
Verse - 95: Indeed, We are sufficient for you against the mockers
Verse - 96: Who make [equal] with Allah another deity. But they are going to know.
Verse - 97: And We already know that your breast is constrained by what they say.
Verse - 98: So exalt [Allah] with praise of your Lord and be of those who prostrate [to Him].
Verse - 99: And worship your Lord until there comes to you the certainty (death).
