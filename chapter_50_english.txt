Chapter - 50: Qaaf

Verse - 1: Qaf. By the honored Qur'an...
Verse - 2: But they wonder that there has come to them a warner from among themselves, and the disbelievers say, "This is an amazing thing.
Verse - 3: When we have died and have become dust, [we will return to life]? That is a distant return."
Verse - 4: We know what the earth diminishes of them, and with Us is a retaining record.
Verse - 5: But they denied the truth when it came to them, so they are in a confused condition.
Verse - 6: Have they not looked at the heaven above them - how We structured it and adorned it and [how] it has no rifts?
Verse - 7: And the earth - We spread it out and cast therein firmly set mountains and made grow therein [something] of every beautiful kind,
Verse - 8: Giving insight and a reminder for every servant who turns [to Allah].
Verse - 9: And We have sent down blessed rain from the sky and made grow thereby gardens and grain from the harvest
Verse - 10: And lofty palm trees having fruit arranged in layers -
Verse - 11: As provision for the servants, and We have given life thereby to a dead land. Thus is the resurrection.
Verse - 12: The people of Noah denied before them, and the companions of the well and Thamud
Verse - 13: And 'Aad and Pharaoh and the brothers of Lot
Verse - 14: And the companions of the thicket and the people of Tubba'. All denied the messengers, so My threat was justly fulfilled.
Verse - 15: Did We fail in the first creation? But they are in confusion over a new creation.
Verse - 16: And We have already created man and know what his soul whispers to him, and We are closer to him than [his] jugular vein
Verse - 17: When the two receivers receive, seated on the right and on the left.
Verse - 18: Man does not utter any word except that with him is an observer prepared [to record].
Verse - 19: And the intoxication of death will bring the truth; that is what you were trying to avoid.
Verse - 20: And the Horn will be blown. That is the Day of [carrying out] the threat.
Verse - 21: And every soul will come, with it a driver and a witness.
Verse - 22: [It will be said], "You were certainly in unmindfulness of this, and We have removed from you your cover, so your sight, this Day, is sharp."
Verse - 23: And his companion, [the angel], will say, "This [record] is what is with me, prepared."
Verse - 24: [Allah will say], "Throw into Hell every obstinate disbeliever,
Verse - 25: Preventer of good, aggressor, and doubter,
Verse - 26: Who made [as equal] with Allah another deity; then throw him into the severe punishment."
Verse - 27: His [devil] companion will say, "Our Lord, I did not make him transgress, but he [himself] was in extreme error."
Verse - 28: [Allah] will say, "Do not dispute before Me, while I had already presented to you the warning.
Verse - 29: The word will not be changed with Me, and never will I be unjust to the servants."
Verse - 30: On the Day We will say to Hell, "Have you been filled?" and it will say, "Are there some more,"
Verse - 31: And Paradise will be brought near to the righteous, not far,
Verse - 32: [It will be said], "This is what you were promised - for every returner [to Allah] and keeper [of His covenant]
Verse - 33: Who feared the Most Merciful unseen and came with a heart returning [in repentance].
Verse - 34: Enter it in peace. This is the Day of Eternity."
Verse - 35: They will have whatever they wish therein, and with Us is more.
Verse - 36: And how many a generation before them did We destroy who were greater than them in [striking] power and had explored throughout the lands. Is there any place of escape?
Verse - 37: Indeed in that is a reminder for whoever has a heart or who listens while he is present [in mind].
Verse - 38: And We did certainly create the heavens and earth and what is between them in six days, and there touched Us no weariness.
Verse - 39: So be patient, [O Muhammad], over what they say and exalt [Allah] with praise of your Lord before the rising of the sun and before its setting,
Verse - 40: And [in part] of the night exalt Him and after prostration.
Verse - 41: And listen on the Day when the Caller will call out from a place that is near -
Verse - 42: The Day they will hear the blast [of the Horn] in truth. That is the Day of Emergence [from the graves].
Verse - 43: Indeed, it is We who give life and cause death, and to Us is the destination
Verse - 44: On the Day the earth breaks away from them [and they emerge] rapidly; that is a gathering easy for Us.
Verse - 45: We are most knowing of what they say, and you are not over them a tyrant. But remind by the Qur'an whoever fears My threat.
