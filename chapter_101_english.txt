Chapter - 101: Al-Qaari'a

Verse - 1: The Striking Calamity -
Verse - 2: What is the Striking Calamity?
Verse - 3: And what can make you know what is the Striking Calamity?
Verse - 4: It is the Day when people will be like moths, dispersed,
Verse - 5: And the mountains will be like wool, fluffed up.
Verse - 6: Then as for one whose scales are heavy [with good deeds],
Verse - 7: He will be in a pleasant life.
Verse - 8: But as for one whose scales are light,
Verse - 9: His refuge will be an abyss.
Verse - 10: And what can make you know what that is?
Verse - 11: It is a Fire, intensely hot.
