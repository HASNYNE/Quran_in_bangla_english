Chapter - 82: Al-Infitaar

Verse - 1: When the sky breaks apart
Verse - 2: And when the stars fall, scattering,
Verse - 3: And when the seas are erupted
Verse - 4: And when the [contents of] graves are scattered,
Verse - 5: A soul will [then] know what it has put forth and kept back.
Verse - 6: O mankind, what has deceived you concerning your Lord, the Generous,
Verse - 7: Who created you, proportioned you, and balanced you?
Verse - 8: In whatever form He willed has He assembled you.
Verse - 9: No! But you deny the Recompense.
Verse - 10: And indeed, [appointed] over you are keepers,
Verse - 11: Noble and recording;
Verse - 12: They know whatever you do.
Verse - 13: Indeed, the righteous will be in pleasure,
Verse - 14: And indeed, the wicked will be in Hellfire.
Verse - 15: They will [enter to] burn therein on the Day of Recompense,
Verse - 16: And never therefrom will they be absent.
Verse - 17: And what can make you know what is the Day of Recompense?
Verse - 18: Then, what can make you know what is the Day of Recompense?
Verse - 19: It is the Day when a soul will not possess for another soul [power to do] a thing; and the command, that Day, is [entirely] with Allah.
