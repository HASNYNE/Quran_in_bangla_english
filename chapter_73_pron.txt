Chapter - 73: Al-Muzzammil (Arabic Pronunciation)

Verse - 1: Yaw ayyuhal muzzammil
Verse - 2: Qumil laila illaa qaleelaa
Verse - 3: Nisfahooo awinqus minhu qaleelaa
Verse - 4: Aw zid 'alaihi wa rattilil Qur'aana tarteela
Verse - 5: Innaa sanulqee 'alika qawalan saqeelaa
Verse - 6: Inn naashi'atal laili hiya ashadddu wat anw wa aqwamu qeelaa
Verse - 7: Inna laka fin nahaari sabhan taweelaa
Verse - 8: Wazkuris ma rabbika wa tabattal ilaihi tabteelaa
Verse - 9: Rabbul mashriqi wal maghriibi laaa ilaaha illaa Huwa fattakhizhu wakeelaa
Verse - 10: Wasbir 'alaa maa yaqoo loona wahjurhum hajran jameelaa
Verse - 11: Wa zarnee walmukaz zibeena ulin na'mati wa mahhilhum qaleelaa
Verse - 12: Inna ladainaaa ankaalanw wa jaheemaa
Verse - 13: Wa ta'aaman zaa ghussa tinw wa'azaaban aleemaa
Verse - 14: Yawma tarjuful ardu waljibaalu wa kaanatil jibaalu kaseebam maheelaa
Verse - 15: Innaa arsalnaaa ilaikum rasoolan shahidan 'alikum kamaaa arsalnaaa ilaa Fir'awna Rasoolna
Verse - 16: Fa'asaa Fir'awnur Rasoola fa akhaznaahu akhzanw wabeelaa
Verse - 17: Fakaifa tattaqoona in kafartum yawmany yaj'alul wildaana sheeba
Verse - 18: Assamaaa'u munfatirum bih; kaana wa'duhoo maf'oola
Verse - 19: Inna haazihee tazkiratun fa man shaaa'at takhaza ilaa Rabbihee sabeelaa
Verse - 20: Inna Rabbaka ya'lamu annaka taqoomu adnaa min sulusa yil laili wa nisfahoo wa sulusahoo wa taaa'ifatum minal lazeena ma'ak; wal laahu yuqaddirul laila wanna haar; 'alima al lan tuhsoohu fataaba 'alaikum faqra'oo maa tayassara minal quraan; 'alima an sa yakoonu minkum mardaa wa aakharoona yadriboona fil ardi yabtaghoona min fadlil laahi wa aakharoona yuqaatiloona fee sabeelil laahi faqra'oo ma tayassara minhu wa aqeemus salaata wa aatuz zakaata wa aqridul laaha qardan hasanaa; wa maa tuqadimoo li anfusikum min khairin tajidoohu 'indal laahi huwa khayranw wa a'zama ajraa; wastaghfirul laahaa innal laaha ghafoorur raheem.
