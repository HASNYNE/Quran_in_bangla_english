Chapter - 111: Al-Masad

Verse - 1: May the hands of Abu Lahab be ruined, and ruined is he.
Verse - 2: His wealth will not avail him or that which he gained.
Verse - 3: He will [enter to] burn in a Fire of [blazing] flame
Verse - 4: And his wife [as well] - the carrier of firewood.
Verse - 5: Around her neck is a rope of [twisted] fiber.
