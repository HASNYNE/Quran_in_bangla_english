Chapter - 21: Al-Anbiyaa (Arabic Pronunciation)

Verse - 1: Iqtaraba linnaasi hisaa buhum wa hum fee ghaflatim mu'ridoon
Verse - 2: Maa yaateehim min zikrim mir Rabbihim muhdasin illas tama'oohu wa hum yal'aboon

Verse - 13: Laa tarkudoo warji'ooo ilaa maaa utriftum feehe wa masaakinikum la'allakum tus'aloon

Verse - 15: Famaa zaalat tilka da'waahum hattaa ja'alnaahum haseedan khaamideen

Verse - 17: Law aradnaaa an nattakhiza lahwal lat takhaznaahu mil ladunnaaa in kunnaa faa'ileen

Verse - 19: Wa lahoo man fis samaawaati wal ard; wa man 'indahoo laa yastakbiroona 'an 'ibaada tihee wa laa yastahsiroon

Verse - 24: Amit takhazoo min doonihee aalihatan qul haatoo burhaanakum haaza zikru mam ma'iya wa zikru man qablee; bal aksaruhum laa ya'lamoonal haqqa fahum mu'ridoon
Verse - 25: Wa maaa arsalnaa min qablika mir Rasoolin illaa nooheee ilaihi annahoo laaa ilaaha illaaa Ana fa'budoon

Verse - 27: Laa yasbiqoonahoo bil qawli wa hum bi amrihee ya'maloon
Verse - 28: Ya'lamu maa baina aideehim wa maa khalfahum wa laa yashfa'oona illaa limanir tadaa wa hum min khash yatihee mushfiqoon
Verse - 29: Wa mai yaqul minhum inneee ilaahum min doonihee fazaalika najzeehi Jahannam; kazaalika najziz zaalimeen
Verse - 30: Awalam yaral lazeena kafarooo annas samaawaati wal arda kaanataa ratqan faftaqnaahumaa wa ja'alnaa minal maaa'i kulla shai'in haiyin afalaa yu'minoon
Verse - 31: Wa ja'alnaa fil ardi rawaasiya an tameeda bihim wa ja'alnaa feehaa fijaajan subulal la'allahum yahtadoon
Verse - 32: Wa ja'alnas samaaa'a saqfam mahfoozanw wa hum 'an Aayaatihaa mu'ridoon
Verse - 33: Wa Huwal lazee khalaqal laila wannahaara washshamsa wal qamara kullun fee falakiny yashbahoon
Verse - 34: Wa maa ja'alnaa libasharim min qablikal khuld; afaimmitta fahumul khaalidoon
Verse - 35: Kullu nafsin zaaa'iqatul mawt; wa nablookum bishsharri walkhairi fitnatanw wa ilainaa turja'oon
Verse - 36: Wa izaa ra aakal lazeena kafarooo iny-yattakhizoonaka illa huzuwaa; ahaazal lazee yazkuru aalihatakum wa hum bi zikrir Rahmaani hum kaafiroon
Verse - 37: Khuliqal insaanu min 'ajal; sa ureekum Aayaatee falaa tasta'jiloon
Verse - 38: Wa yaqooloona mataa haazal wa'du in kuntum saadiqeen
Verse - 39: Law ya'lamul lazeena kafaroo heena laa yakuffoona 'anw wujoohihimun Naara wa laa 'an zuhoorihim wa laa hum yunsaroon
Verse - 40: Bal taateehim baghtatan fatabhatuhum falaa yastatee'oona raddahaa wa laa hum yunzaroon
Verse - 41: Wa laqadis tuhzi'a bi-Rusulim min qablika fahaaqa billazeena sakhiroo minhum maa kaanoo bihee yastahzi'oon
Verse - 42: Qul mai yakla 'ukum billaili wannahaari minar Rahmaan; bal hum 'an zikri Rabbihim mu'ridoon
Verse - 43: Am lahum aalihatun tamna'ulum min dooninaa; laa yastatee'oona nasra anfusihim wa laa hum minnna yus-haboon
Verse - 44: Bal matta'naa haaa'ulaaa'i wa aabaaa'ahum hattaa taala 'alaihimul 'umur; afalaa yarawna anna naatil arda nanqusuhaa min atraafihaa; afahumul ghaaliboon
Verse - 45: Qul innamaaa unzirukum bilwahyi; wa laa yasma'us summud du'aaa'a izaa maa yunzaroon

Verse - 47: Wa nada'ul mawaazeenal qista li Yawmil Qiyaamati falaa tuzlamu nafsun shai'aa; wa in kaana misqaala habbatim min khardalin atainaa bihaa; wa kafaa binaa haasibeen
Verse - 48: Wa laqad aatainaa Moosa wa haaroonal Furqaana wa diyaa'anw wa zikral lilmuttaqeen

Verse - 59: Qaaloo man fa'ala haazaa bi aalihatinaaa innahoo laminaz zaalimeen
Verse - 60: Qaaloo sami'naa fatany yazkuruhum yuqaalu lahooo Ibraaheem

Verse - 63: Qaala bal fa'alahoo kabeeruhum haazaa fas'aloohum in kaanoo yantiqoon
Verse - 64: Faraja'ooo ilaaa anfusihim faqaalooo innakum antumuz zaalimoon
Verse - 65: Summa nukisoo 'alaa ru'oosihim laqad 'alimta maa haaa'ulaaa'i yantiqoon
Verse - 66: Qaala afata'budoona min doonil laahi maa laa yanfa'ukum shai'anw wa laa yadurrukum
Verse - 67: Uffil lakum wa limaa ta'budoona min doonil laah; afalaa ta'qiloon
Verse - 68: Qaaloo harriqooho wansurooo aalihatakum in kuntum faa'ileen
Verse - 69: Qulnaa yaa naaru koonee bardanw wa salaaman 'alaaa Ibraaheem
Verse - 70: Wa araadoo bihee kaidan faja'alnaahumul akhsareen
Verse - 71: Wa najjainaahu wa Lootan ilal ardil latee baaraknaa feehaa lil 'aalameen

Verse - 74: Wa Lootan aatainaahu hukmanw wa 'ilmanw wa najjainaahu minal qaryatil latee kaanat ta'malul khabaaa'is; innahum kaanoo qawma saw'in faasiqeen
