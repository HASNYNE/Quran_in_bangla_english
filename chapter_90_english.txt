Chapter - 90: Al-Balad

Verse - 1: I swear by this city, Makkah -
Verse - 2: And you, [O Muhammad], are free of restriction in this city -
Verse - 3: And [by] the father and that which was born [of him],
Verse - 4: We have certainly created man into hardship.
Verse - 5: Does he think that never will anyone overcome him?
Verse - 6: He says, "I have spent wealth in abundance."
Verse - 7: Does he think that no one has seen him?
Verse - 8: Have We not made for him two eyes?
Verse - 9: And a tongue and two lips?
Verse - 10: And have shown him the two ways?
Verse - 11: But he has not broken through the difficult pass.
Verse - 12: And what can make you know what is [breaking through] the difficult pass?
Verse - 13: It is the freeing of a slave
Verse - 14: Or feeding on a day of severe hunger
Verse - 15: An orphan of near relationship
Verse - 16: Or a needy person in misery
Verse - 17: And then being among those who believed and advised one another to patience and advised one another to compassion.
Verse - 18: Those are the companions of the right.
Verse - 19: But they who disbelieved in Our signs - those are the companions of the left.
Verse - 20: Over them will be fire closed in.
