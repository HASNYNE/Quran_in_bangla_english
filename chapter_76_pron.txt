Chapter - 76: Al-Insaan (Arabic Pronunciation)

Verse - 1: Hal ataa 'alal insaani heenum minad dahri lam yakun shai'am mazkooraa
Verse - 2: Innaa khalaqnal insaana min nutfatin amshaajin nabta leehi faja'alnaahu samee'am baseeraa
Verse - 3: Innaa hadainaahus sabeela immaa shaakiranw wa immaa kafoora
Verse - 4: Innaaa a'tadnaa lilkaa fireena salaasila wa aghlaalanw wa sa'eeraa
Verse - 5: innal abraara yashra boona min kaasin kaana mizaa juhaa kaafooraa
Verse - 6: 'Aynany yashrabu bihaa 'ibaadul laahi yafajjiroonahaa tafjeeraa
Verse - 7: Yoofoona binnazri wa yakhaafoona yawman kaana sharruhoo mustateeraa
Verse - 8: Wa yut''imoonat ta'aama 'alaa hubbihee miskeenanw wa yatemanw wa aseeraa
Verse - 9: Innaamaa nut'imukum li wajhil laahi laa nureedu minkum jazaaa'anw wa laa shukooraa
Verse - 10: Innaa nakhaafu mir Rabbinna Yawman 'aboosan qamtareeraa
Verse - 11: Fa waqaahumul laahu sharra zaalikal yawmi wa laqqaahum nadratanw wa surooraa
Verse - 12: Wa jazaahum bimaa sabaroo janatanw wa hareeraa
Verse - 13: muttaki'eena feeha 'alal araaa 'iki laa yarawna feehaa shamsanw wa laa zamhareeraa
Verse - 14: Wa daaniyatan 'alaihim zilaaluhaa wa zullilat qutoofu haa tazleela
Verse - 15: Wa yutaafu 'alaihim bi aaniyatim min fiddatinw wa akwaabin kaanat qawaareeraa
Verse - 16: Qawaareera min fiddatin qaddaroohaa taqdeeraa
Verse - 17: Wa yusawna feehaa kaasan kaana mizaajuhaa zanjabeelaa
Verse - 18: 'Aynan feeha tusammaa salsabeelaa
Verse - 19: Wa yatoofu 'alaihim wildaanum mukhalladoona izaa ra aytahum hasibtahum lu'lu 'am mansoora
Verse - 20: Wa izaa ra ayta summa ra ayta na'eemanw wa mulkan kabeera
Verse - 21: 'Aaliyahum siyaabu sundusin khudrunw wa istabraq, wa hullooo asaawira min fiddatinw wa saqaahum Rabbuhum sharaaban tahooraa
Verse - 22: Innaa haazaa kaana lakum jazz 'anw wa kaana sa'yukum mashkooraa
Verse - 23: Innaa nahnu nazzalnaa 'alaikal quraana tanzeelaa
Verse - 24: Fasbir lihukmi Rabbika wa laa tuti' minhum aasiman aw kafooraa
Verse - 25: Wazkuris ma Rabbika bukratanw wa aseelaa
Verse - 26: Wa minal laili fasjud lahoo wa sabbihhu lailan taweelaa
Verse - 27: Inna haaa'ulaa'i yuhibboona 'aajilata wa yazaroona waraaa'ahum yawman saqeelaa
Verse - 28: Nahnu khalaqnaahum wa shadadnaaa asrahum wa izaa shi'naa baddalnaaa amsaala hum tabdeelaa
Verse - 29: Inna haazihee tazkiratun fa man shaaa'at takhaza ilaa rabbihee sabeela
Verse - 30: Wa maa tashaaa'oona illaa anyyashaaa'al laah; innal laahaa kaana'Aleeman Hakeema
Verse - 31: Yudkhilu mai yashaaa'u fee rahmatih; wazzaalimeena a'adda lahum 'azaaban aleemaa
