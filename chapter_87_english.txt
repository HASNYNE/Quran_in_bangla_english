Chapter - 87: Al-A'laa

Verse - 1: Exalt the name of your Lord, the Most High,
Verse - 2: Who created and proportioned
Verse - 3: And who destined and [then] guided
Verse - 4: And who brings out the pasture
Verse - 5: And [then] makes it black stubble.
Verse - 6: We will make you recite, [O Muhammad], and you will not forget,
Verse - 7: Except what Allah should will. Indeed, He knows what is declared and what is hidden.
Verse - 8: And We will ease you toward ease.
Verse - 9: So remind, if the reminder should benefit;
Verse - 10: He who fears [Allah] will be reminded.
Verse - 11: But the wretched one will avoid it -
Verse - 12: [He] who will [enter and] burn in the greatest Fire,
Verse - 13: Neither dying therein nor living.
Verse - 14: He has certainly succeeded who purifies himself
Verse - 15: And mentions the name of his Lord and prays.
Verse - 16: But you prefer the worldly life,
Verse - 17: While the Hereafter is better and more enduring.
Verse - 18: Indeed, this is in the former scriptures,
Verse - 19: The scriptures of Abraham and Moses.
