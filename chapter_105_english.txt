Chapter - 105: Al-Fil

Verse - 1: Have you not considered, [O Muhammad], how your Lord dealt with the companions of the elephant?
Verse - 2: Did He not make their plan into misguidance?
Verse - 3: And He sent against them birds in flocks,
Verse - 4: Striking them with stones of hard clay,
Verse - 5: And He made them like eaten straw.
