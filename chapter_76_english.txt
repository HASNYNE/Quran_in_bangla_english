Chapter - 76: Al-Insaan

Verse - 1: Has there [not] come upon man a period of time when he was not a thing [even] mentioned?
Verse - 2: Indeed, We created man from a sperm-drop mixture that We may try him; and We made him hearing and seeing.
Verse - 3: Indeed, We guided him to the way, be he grateful or be he ungrateful.
Verse - 4: Indeed, We have prepared for the disbelievers chains and shackles and a blaze.
Verse - 5: Indeed, the righteous will drink from a cup [of wine] whose mixture is of Kafur,
Verse - 6: A spring of which the [righteous] servants of Allah will drink; they will make it gush forth in force [and abundance].
Verse - 7: They [are those who] fulfill [their] vows and fear a Day whose evil will be widespread.
Verse - 8: And they give food in spite of love for it to the needy, the orphan, and the captive,
Verse - 9: [Saying], "We feed you only for the countenance of Allah. We wish not from you reward or gratitude.
Verse - 10: Indeed, We fear from our Lord a Day austere and distressful."
Verse - 11: So Allah will protect them from the evil of that Day and give them radiance and happiness
Verse - 12: And will reward them for what they patiently endured [with] a garden [in Paradise] and silk [garments].
Verse - 13: [They will be] reclining therein on adorned couches. They will not see therein any [burning] sun or [freezing] cold.
Verse - 14: And near above them are its shades, and its [fruit] to be picked will be lowered in compliance.
Verse - 15: And there will be circulated among them vessels of silver and cups having been [created] clear [as glass],
Verse - 16: Clear glasses [made] from silver of which they have determined the measure.
Verse - 17: And they will be given to drink a cup [of wine] whose mixture is of ginger
Verse - 18: [From] a fountain within Paradise named Salsabeel.
Verse - 19: There will circulate among them young boys made eternal. When you see them, you would think them [as beautiful as] scattered pearls.
Verse - 20: And when you look there [in Paradise], you will see pleasure and great dominion.
Verse - 21: Upon the inhabitants will be green garments of fine silk and brocade. And they will be adorned with bracelets of silver, and their Lord will give them a purifying drink.
Verse - 22: [And it will be said], "Indeed, this is for you a reward, and your effort has been appreciated."
Verse - 23: Indeed, it is We who have sent down to you, [O Muhammad], the Qur'an progressively.
Verse - 24: So be patient for the decision of your Lord and do not obey from among them a sinner or ungrateful [disbeliever].
Verse - 25: And mention the name of your Lord [in prayer] morning and evening
Verse - 26: And during the night prostrate to Him and exalt Him a long [part of the] night.
Verse - 27: Indeed, these [disbelievers] love the immediate and leave behind them a grave Day.
Verse - 28: We have created them and strengthened their forms, and when We will, We can change their likenesses with [complete] alteration.
Verse - 29: Indeed, this is a reminder, so he who wills may take to his Lord a way.
Verse - 30: And you do not will except that Allah wills. Indeed, Allah is ever Knowing and Wise.
Verse - 31: He admits whom He wills into His mercy; but the wrongdoers - He has prepared for them a painful punishment.
