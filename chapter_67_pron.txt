Chapter - 67: Al-Mulk (Arabic Pronunciation)

Verse - 1: Tabaarakal lazee biyadihil mulku wa huwa 'alaa kulli shai-in qadeer
Verse - 2: Allazee khalaqal mawta walhayaata liyabluwakum ayyukum ahsanu 'amalaa; wa huwal 'azeezul ghafoor
Verse - 3: Allazee khalaqa sab'a samaawaatin tibaaqam maa taraa fee khalqir rahmaani min tafaawutin farji'il basara hal taraa min futoor
Verse - 4: Summar ji'il basara karrataini yanqalib ilaikal basaru khaasi'anw wa huwa haseer
Verse - 5: Wa laqad zaiyannas samaaa'ad dunyaa bimasaa beeha wa ja'alnaahaa rujoomal lish shayaateeni wa a'tadnaa lahum 'azaabas sa'eer
Verse - 6: Wa lillazeena kafaroo bi rabbihim 'azaabu jahannama wa bi'sal maseer
Verse - 7: Izaaa ulqoo feehaa sami'oo lahaa shaheeqanw wa hiya tafoor
Verse - 8: Takaadu tamayyazu minal ghaizi kullamaaa uliqya feehaa fawjun sa alahum khazanatuhaaa alam yaatikum nazeer
Verse - 9: Qaaloo balaa qad jaaa'anaa nazeerun fakazzabnaa wa qulnaa maa nazzalal laahu min shai in in antum illaa fee dalaalin kabeer
Verse - 10: Wa qaaloo law kunnaa nasma'u awna'qilu maa kunnaa feee as haabis sa'eer
Verse - 11: Fa'tarafoo bizambihim fasuhqal li as haabis sa'eer
Verse - 12: Innal lazeena yakhshawna rabbahum bilghaibi lahum maghfiratunw wa ajrun kabeer
Verse - 13: Wa asirroo qawlakum awijharoo bihee innahoo 'aleemum bizaatis sudoor
Verse - 14: Alaa ya'lamu man khalaq wa huwal lateeful khabeer
Verse - 15: Huwal lazee ja'ala lakumul arda zaloolan famshoo fee manaakibihaa wa kuloo mir rizqihee wa ilaihin nushoor
Verse - 16: 'A-amintum man fissamaaa'i aiyakhsifa bi kumul arda fa izaa hiya tamoor
Verse - 17: Am amintum man fissamaaa'i ai yursila 'alaikum haasiban fasata'lamoona kaifa nazeer
Verse - 18: Wa laqad kazzabal lazeena min qablihim fakaifa kaana nakeer
Verse - 19: Awalam yaraw ilat tairi fawqahum saaaffaatinw wa yaqbidn; maa yumsikuhunna illaar rahmaan; innahoo bikulli shai im baseer
Verse - 20: Amman haazal lazee huwa jundul lakum yansurukum min doonir rahmaan; inilkaafiroona illaa fee ghuroor
Verse - 21: Amman haazal lazee yarzuqukum in amsaka rizqah; bal lajjoo fee 'utuwwinw wa nufoor
Verse - 22: Afamai yamshee mukibban 'alaa wajhihee ahdaaa ammany yamshee sawiyyan 'alaa siratim mustaqeem
Verse - 23: Qul huwal lazee ansha akum wa ja'ala lakumus sam'a wal absaara wal af'idata qaleelam maa tashkuroon
Verse - 24: Qul huwal lazee zara akum fil ardi wa ilaihi tuhsharoon
Verse - 25: Wa yaqooloona mataa haazal wa'du in kuntum saadiqeen
Verse - 26: Qul innamal 'ilmu 'indallaahi wa innamaaa ana nazeerum mubeen
Verse - 27: Falaammaa ra-awhu zulfatan seee'at wujoohul lazeena kafaroo wa qeela haazal lazee kuntum bihee tadda'oon
Verse - 28: Qul ara'aytum in ahlaka niyal laahu wa mam ma'iya aw rahimanaa famai-yujeerul kaafireena min 'azaabin aleem
Verse - 29: Qul huwar rahmaanu aamannaa bihee wa 'alaihi tawakkalnaa fasata'lamoona man huwa fee dalaalim mubeen
Verse - 30: Qul ara'aytum in asbaha maaa'ukum ghawran famai yaateekum bimaaa'im ma'een
