Chapter - 81: At-Takwir

Verse - 1: When the sun is wrapped up [in darkness]
Verse - 2: And when the stars fall, dispersing,
Verse - 3: And when the mountains are removed
Verse - 4: And when full-term she-camels are neglected
Verse - 5: And when the wild beasts are gathered
Verse - 6: And when the seas are filled with flame
Verse - 7: And when the souls are paired
Verse - 8: And when the girl [who was] buried alive is asked
Verse - 9: For what sin she was killed
Verse - 10: And when the pages are made public
Verse - 11: And when the sky is stripped away
Verse - 12: And when Hellfire is set ablaze
Verse - 13: And when Paradise is brought near,
Verse - 14: A soul will [then] know what it has brought [with it].
Verse - 15: So I swear by the retreating stars -
Verse - 16: Those that run [their courses] and disappear -
Verse - 17: And by the night as it closes in
Verse - 18: And by the dawn when it breathes
Verse - 19: [That] indeed, the Qur'an is a word [conveyed by] a noble messenger
Verse - 20: [Who is] possessed of power and with the Owner of the Throne, secure [in position],
Verse - 21: Obeyed there [in the heavens] and trustworthy.
Verse - 22: And your companion is not [at all] mad.
Verse - 23: And he has already seen Gabriel in the clear horizon.
Verse - 24: And Muhammad is not a withholder of [knowledge of] the unseen.
Verse - 25: And the Qur'an is not the word of a devil, expelled [from the heavens].
Verse - 26: So where are you going?
Verse - 27: It is not except a reminder to the worlds
Verse - 28: For whoever wills among you to take a right course.
Verse - 29: And you do not will except that Allah wills - Lord of the worlds.
