Chapter - 86: At-Taariq

Verse - 1: By the sky and the night comer -
Verse - 2: And what can make you know what is the night comer?
Verse - 3: It is the piercing star -
Verse - 4: There is no soul but that it has over it a protector.
Verse - 5: So let man observe from what he was created.
Verse - 6: He was created from a fluid, ejected,
Verse - 7: Emerging from between the backbone and the ribs.
Verse - 8: Indeed, Allah, to return him [to life], is Able.
Verse - 9: The Day when secrets will be put on trial,
Verse - 10: Then man will have no power or any helper.
Verse - 11: By the sky which returns [rain]
Verse - 12: And [by] the earth which cracks open,
Verse - 13: Indeed, the Qur'an is a decisive statement,
Verse - 14: And it is not amusement.
Verse - 15: Indeed, they are planning a plan,
Verse - 16: But I am planning a plan.
Verse - 17: So allow time for the disbelievers. Leave them awhile.
