Chapter - 77: Al-Mursalaat

Verse - 1: By those [winds] sent forth in gusts
Verse - 2: And the winds that blow violently
Verse - 3: And [by] the winds that spread [clouds]
Verse - 4: And those [angels] who bring criterion
Verse - 5: And those [angels] who deliver a message
Verse - 6: As justification or warning,
Verse - 7: Indeed, what you are promised is to occur.
Verse - 8: So when the stars are obliterated
Verse - 9: And when the heaven is opened
Verse - 10: And when the mountains are blown away
Verse - 11: And when the messengers' time has come...
Verse - 12: For what Day was it postponed?
Verse - 13: For the Day of Judgement.
Verse - 14: And what can make you know what is the Day of Judgement?
Verse - 15: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 16: Did We not destroy the former peoples?
Verse - 17: Then We will follow them with the later ones.
Verse - 18: Thus do We deal with the criminals.
Verse - 19: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 20: Did We not create you from a liquid disdained?
Verse - 21: And We placed it in a firm lodging
Verse - 22: For a known extent.
Verse - 23: And We determined [it], and excellent [are We] to determine.
Verse - 24: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 25: Have We not made the earth a container
Verse - 26: Of the living and the dead?
Verse - 27: And We placed therein lofty, firmly set mountains and have given you to drink sweet water.
Verse - 28: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 29: [They will be told], "Proceed to that which you used to deny.
Verse - 30: Proceed to a shadow [of smoke] having three columns
Verse - 31: [But having] no cool shade and availing not against the flame."
Verse - 32: Indeed, it throws sparks [as huge] as a fortress,
Verse - 33: As if they were yellowish [black] camels.
Verse - 34: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 35: This is a Day they will not speak,
Verse - 36: Nor will it be permitted for them to make an excuse.
Verse - 37: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 38: This is the Day of Judgement; We will have assembled you and the former peoples.
Verse - 39: So if you have a plan, then plan against Me.
Verse - 40: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 41: Indeed, the righteous will be among shades and springs
Verse - 42: And fruits from whatever they desire,
Verse - 43: [Being told], "Eat and drink in satisfaction for what you used to do."
Verse - 44: Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.
Verse - 45: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 46: [O disbelievers], eat and enjoy yourselves a little; indeed, you are criminals.
Verse - 47: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 48: And when it is said to them, "Bow [in prayer]," they do not bow.
Verse - 49: Woe, that Day, to the deniers.
Verse - 50: Then in what statement after the Qur'an will they believe?
