Chapter - 104: Al-Humaza

Verse - 1: Woe to every scorner and mocker
Verse - 2: Who collects wealth and [continuously] counts it.
Verse - 3: He thinks that his wealth will make him immortal.
Verse - 4: No! He will surely be thrown into the Crusher.
Verse - 5: And what can make you know what is the Crusher?
Verse - 6: It is the fire of Allah, [eternally] fueled,
Verse - 7: Which mounts directed at the hearts.
Verse - 8: Indeed, Hellfire will be closed down upon them
Verse - 9: In extended columns.
