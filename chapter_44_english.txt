Chapter - 44: Ad-Dukhaan

Verse - 1: Ha, Meem.
Verse - 2: By the clear Book,
Verse - 3: Indeed, We sent it down during a blessed night. Indeed, We were to warn [mankind].
Verse - 4: On that night is made distinct every precise matter -
Verse - 5: [Every] matter [proceeding] from Us. Indeed, We were to send [a messenger]
Verse - 6: As mercy from your Lord. Indeed, He is the Hearing, the Knowing.
Verse - 7: Lord of the heavens and the earth and that between them, if you would be certain.
Verse - 8: There is no deity except Him; He gives life and causes death. [He is] your Lord and the Lord of your first forefathers.
Verse - 9: But they are in doubt, amusing themselves.
Verse - 10: Then watch for the Day when the sky will bring a visible smoke.
Verse - 11: Covering the people; this is a painful torment.
Verse - 12: [They will say], "Our Lord, remove from us the torment; indeed, we are believers."
Verse - 13: How will there be for them a reminder [at that time]? And there had come to them a clear Messenger.
Verse - 14: Then they turned away from him and said, "[He was] taught [and is] a madman."
Verse - 15: Indeed, We will remove the torment for a little. Indeed, you [disbelievers] will return [to disbelief].
Verse - 16: The Day We will strike with the greatest assault, indeed, We will take retribution.
Verse - 17: And We had already tried before them the people of Pharaoh, and there came to them a noble messenger,
Verse - 18: [Saying], "Render to me the servants of Allah. Indeed, I am to you a trustworthy messenger,"
Verse - 19: And [saying], "Be not haughty with Allah. Indeed, I have come to you with clear authority.
Verse - 20: And indeed, I have sought refuge in my Lord and your Lord, lest you stone me.
Verse - 21: But if you do not believe me, then leave me alone."
Verse - 22: And [finally] he called to his Lord that these were a criminal people.
Verse - 23: [Allah said], "Then set out with My servants by night. Indeed, you are to be pursued.
Verse - 24: And leave the sea in stillness. Indeed, they are an army to be drowned."
Verse - 25: How much they left behind of gardens and springs
Verse - 26: And crops and noble sites
Verse - 27: And comfort wherein they were amused.
Verse - 28: Thus. And We caused to inherit it another people.
Verse - 29: And the heaven and earth wept not for them, nor were they reprieved.
Verse - 30: And We certainly saved the Children of Israel from the humiliating torment -
Verse - 31: From Pharaoh. Indeed, he was a haughty one among the transgressors.
Verse - 32: And We certainly chose them by knowledge over [all] the worlds.
Verse - 33: And We gave them of signs that in which there was a clear trial.
Verse - 34: Indeed, these [disbelievers] are saying,
Verse - 35: "There is not but our first death, and we will not be resurrected.
Verse - 36: Then bring [back] our forefathers, if you should be truthful."
Verse - 37: Are they better or the people of Tubba' and those before them? We destroyed them, [for] indeed, they were criminals.
Verse - 38: And We did not create the heavens and earth and that between them in play.
Verse - 39: We did not create them except in truth, but most of them do not know.
Verse - 40: Indeed, the Day of Judgement is the appointed time for them all -
Verse - 41: The Day when no relation will avail a relation at all, nor will they be helped -
Verse - 42: Except those [believers] on whom Allah has mercy. Indeed, He is the Exalted in Might, the Merciful.
Verse - 43: Indeed, the tree of zaqqum
Verse - 44: Is food for the sinful.
Verse - 45: Like murky oil, it boils within bellies
Verse - 46: Like the boiling of scalding water.
Verse - 47: [It will be commanded], "Seize him and drag him into the midst of the Hellfire,
Verse - 48: Then pour over his head from the torment of scalding water."
Verse - 49: [It will be said], "Taste! Indeed, you are the honored, the noble!
Verse - 50: Indeed, this is what you used to dispute."
Verse - 51: Indeed, the righteous will be in a secure place;
Verse - 52: Within gardens and springs,
Verse - 53: Wearing [garments of] fine silk and brocade, facing each other.
Verse - 54: Thus. And We will marry them to fair women with large, [beautiful] eyes.
Verse - 55: They will call therein for every [kind of] fruit - safe and secure.
Verse - 56: They will not taste death therein except the first death, and He will have protected them from the punishment of Hellfire
Verse - 57: As bounty from your Lord. That is what is the great attainment.
Verse - 58: And indeed, We have eased the Qur'an in your tongue that they might be reminded.
Verse - 59: So watch, [O Muhammad]; indeed, they are watching [for your end].
