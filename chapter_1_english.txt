Chapter - 1: Al-Faatiha

Verse - 1: In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful.
Verse - 2: [All] praise is [due] to Allah, Lord of the worlds -
Verse - 3: The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful,
Verse - 4: Sovereign of the Day of Recompense.
Verse - 5: It is You we worship and You we ask for help.
Verse - 6: Guide us to the straight path -
Verse - 7: The path of those upon whom You have bestowed favor, not of those who have evoked [Your] anger or of those who are astray.
