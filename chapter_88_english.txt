Chapter - 88: Al-Ghaashiya

Verse - 1: Has there reached you the report of the Overwhelming [event]?
Verse - 2: [Some] faces, that Day, will be humbled,
Verse - 3: Working [hard] and exhausted.
Verse - 4: They will [enter to] burn in an intensely hot Fire.
Verse - 5: They will be given drink from a boiling spring.
Verse - 6: For them there will be no food except from a poisonous, thorny plant
Verse - 7: Which neither nourishes nor avails against hunger.
Verse - 8: [Other] faces, that Day, will show pleasure.
Verse - 9: With their effort [they are] satisfied
Verse - 10: In an elevated garden,
Verse - 11: Wherein they will hear no unsuitable speech.
Verse - 12: Within it is a flowing spring.
Verse - 13: Within it are couches raised high
Verse - 14: And cups put in place
Verse - 15: And cushions lined up
Verse - 16: And carpets spread around.
Verse - 17: Then do they not look at the camels - how they are created?
Verse - 18: And at the sky - how it is raised?
Verse - 19: And at the mountains - how they are erected?
Verse - 20: And at the earth - how it is spread out?
Verse - 21: So remind, [O Muhammad]; you are only a reminder.
Verse - 22: You are not over them a controller.
Verse - 23: However, he who turns away and disbelieves -
Verse - 24: Then Allah will punish him with the greatest punishment.
Verse - 25: Indeed, to Us is their return.
Verse - 26: Then indeed, upon Us is their account.
