Chapter - 72: Al-Jinn (Arabic Pronunciation)

Verse - 1: Qul oohiya ilaiya annna hustama'a nafarum minal jinnni faqaalooo innaa sami'naa quraanan 'ajabaa
Verse - 2: Yahdeee ilar rushdi fa aamannaa bihee wa lan nushrika bi rabbinaaa ahadaa
Verse - 3: Wa annahoo Ta'aalaa jaddu Rabbinaa mat takhaza saahibatanw wa la waladaa
Verse - 4: Wa annahoo kaana yaqoolu safeehunaa 'alal laahi shatataa
Verse - 5: Wa annaa zanannaaa al lan taqoolal insu wal jinnu 'alal laahi kazibaa
Verse - 6: Wa annahoo kaana rijaa lum minal insi ya'oozoona birijaalim minal jinni fazaa doohum rahaqaa
Verse - 7: Wa annahum zannoo kamaa zanantum al lany yab'asal laahu ahadaa
Verse - 8: Wa annaa lamasnas sa maaa'a fa wajadnaahaa muli'at harasan shadeedanw wa shuhubaa
Verse - 9: Wa annaa kunnaa naq'udu minhaa maqaa'ida lis'sam'i famany yastami'il aana yajid lahoo shihaabar rasada
Verse - 10: Wa annaa laa nadreee asharrun ureeda biman fil ardi am araada bihim rabbuhum rashadaa
Verse - 11: Wa annaa minnas saalihoona wa minnaa doona zaalika kunnaa taraaa'ilqa qidadaa
Verse - 12: Wa annaa zanannaaa al lan nu'jizal laaha fil ardi wa lan nu'jizahoo harabaa
Verse - 13: Wa annaa lammaa sami'nal hudaaa aamannaa bihee famany yu'mim bi rabbihee falaa yakhaafu bakhsanw wa laa rahaqaa
Verse - 14: Wa annaa minnal muslimoona wa minnal qaasitoona faman aslama fa ulaaa'ika taharraw rashadaa
Verse - 15: Wa ammal qaasitoona fa kaanoo li jahannama hatabaa
Verse - 16: Wa alla wis taqaamoo 'alat tareeqati la asqaynaahum maa'an ghadaqaa
Verse - 17: Linaftinahum feeh; wa many yu'rid 'an zikri rabbihee yasluk hu 'azaaban sa'adaa
Verse - 18: Wa annal masaajida lillaahi falaa tad'oo ma'al laahi ahadaa
Verse - 19: Wa annahoo lammaa qaama 'adul laahi yad'oohu kaadoo yakoonoona 'alaihi libadaa
Verse - 20: Qul innamaaa ad'oo rabbee wa laaa ushriku biheee ahadaa
Verse - 21: Qul innee laaa amliku lakum darranw wa laa rashadaa
Verse - 22: Qul innee lany yujeeranee minal laahi ahad, wa lan ajida min doonihee multahadaa
Verse - 23: Illaa balaagham minal laahi wa risaalaatih; wa many ya'sil laaha wa rasoolahoo fa inna lahoo naara jahannama khaalideena feehaaa abadaa
Verse - 24: Hattaaa izaa ra aw maa yoo'adoona fasaya'lamoona man ad'afu naasiranw wa aqallu 'adadaa
Verse - 25: Qul in adreee a qareebum maa too'adoona am yaj'alu lahoo rabbeee amadaa
Verse - 26: 'Aalimul ghaibi falaa yuzhiru alaa ghaibiheee ahadaa
Verse - 27: Illaa manir tadaa mir rasoolin fa innahoo yasluku mim baini yadihi wa min khalfihee rasadaa
Verse - 28: Liya'lama an qad ablaghoo risaalaati rabbihim wa ahaata bima ladihim wa ahsaa kulla shai'in 'adadaa
