Chapter - 70: Al-Ma'aarij

Verse - 1: A supplicant asked for a punishment bound to happen
Verse - 2: To the disbelievers; of it there is no preventer.
Verse - 3: [It is] from Allah, owner of the ways of ascent.
Verse - 4: The angels and the Spirit will ascend to Him during a Day the extent of which is fifty thousand years.
Verse - 5: So be patient with gracious patience.
Verse - 6: Indeed, they see it [as] distant,
Verse - 7: But We see it [as] near.
Verse - 8: On the Day the sky will be like murky oil,
Verse - 9: And the mountains will be like wool,
Verse - 10: And no friend will ask [anything of] a friend,
Verse - 11: They will be shown each other. The criminal will wish that he could be ransomed from the punishment of that Day by his children
Verse - 12: And his wife and his brother
Verse - 13: And his nearest kindred who shelter him
Verse - 14: And whoever is on earth entirely [so] then it could save him.
Verse - 15: No! Indeed, it is the Flame [of Hell],
Verse - 16: A remover of exteriors.
Verse - 17: It invites he who turned his back [on truth] and went away [from obedience]
Verse - 18: And collected [wealth] and hoarded.
Verse - 19: Indeed, mankind was created anxious:
Verse - 20: When evil touches him, impatient,
Verse - 21: And when good touches him, withholding [of it],
Verse - 22: Except the observers of prayer -
Verse - 23: Those who are constant in their prayer
Verse - 24: And those within whose wealth is a known right
Verse - 25: For the petitioner and the deprived -
Verse - 26: And those who believe in the Day of Recompense
Verse - 27: And those who are fearful of the punishment of their Lord -
Verse - 28: Indeed, the punishment of their Lord is not that from which one is safe -
Verse - 29: And those who guard their private parts
Verse - 30: Except from their wives or those their right hands possess, for indeed, they are not to be blamed -
Verse - 31: But whoever seeks beyond that, then they are the transgressors -
Verse - 32: And those who are to their trusts and promises attentive
Verse - 33: And those who are in their testimonies upright
Verse - 34: And those who [carefully] maintain their prayer:
Verse - 35: They will be in gardens, honored.
Verse - 36: So what is [the matter] with those who disbelieve, hastening [from] before you, [O Muhammad],
Verse - 37: [To sit] on [your] right and [your] left in separate groups?
Verse - 38: Does every person among them aspire to enter a garden of pleasure?
Verse - 39: No! Indeed, We have created them from that which they know.
Verse - 40: So I swear by the Lord of [all] risings and settings that indeed We are able
Verse - 41: To replace them with better than them; and We are not to be outdone.
Verse - 42: So leave them to converse vainly and amuse themselves until they meet their Day which they are promised -
Verse - 43: The Day they will emerge from the graves rapidly as if they were, toward an erected idol, hastening.
Verse - 44: Their eyes humbled, humiliation will cover them. That is the Day which they had been promised.
